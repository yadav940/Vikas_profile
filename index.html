Who I Am

About Me

I am Vikas Yadav, a final-year B.Pharm student at the Institute of Pharmacy, Dr. Rammanohar Lohia Avadh University (RMLAU), Ayodhya — graduating in 2026. My pharmaceutical journey combines rigorous academic training with real-world industrial and clinical exposure, underpinned by a passion for quality-driven science.

My industrial foundation was built at Vellinton Healthcare, Himachal Pradesh — a WHO-GMP certified facility. Over five weeks I worked across Production and Quality Control departments: operating HPLC for raw material and finished-product assays, preparing and evaluating mobile phases per pharmacopoeial methods, managing Batch Manufacturing Records (BMR), monitoring stability chambers, evaluating packing materials, and performing autoclave sterilisation in the microbiology section.

My earlier clinical internship at District Women Hospital, Azamgarh sharpened patient-facing skills — prescription verification, drug dispensing, controlled-substance inventory, and support for clinical pharmacy teams in medication reconciliation and patient counselling.

Academically I authored a review on Hepatoprotective Mechanisms of Curcumin and led formulation of a Herbal Mosquito Repellent Gel (Aloe Vera · Citronella · Neem · Turmeric). My scientific communication earned me First Prize at the World Pharmacist Day Poster Competition 2023 at RMLAU. I hold an NSQF Level 5 AI-in-Pharma certification from Skill India & LSSSDC, positioning me at the intersection of traditional pharmaceutical science and AI-driven drug discovery.
