About Me

I am Vikas Yadav, a final-year B.Pharm student at the Institute of Pharmacy, Dr. Rammanohar Lohia Avadh University (RMLAU), Ayodhya — graduating in 2026. My pharmaceutical journey is shaped by a deep commitment to quality, scientific rigor, and a vision to integrate emerging technologies into traditional drug development.

My industrial foundation was forged during a rigorous training programme at Vellinton Healthcare, Himachal Pradesh — a WHO-GMP certified pharmaceutical manufacturing facility. Working within the Production and Quality Control departments, I gained direct hands-on experience operating HPLC instruments for raw material and finished product analysis, preparing and evaluating mobile phases using pharmacopoeial methods, managing Batch Manufacturing Records (BMR), conducting stability chamber monitoring, packing material evaluation, and performing autoclave sterilization in the microbiology section. This training gave me a complete understanding of pharmaceutical manufacturing from raw input to product release.

Prior to that, my clinical internship at District Women Hospital, Azamgarh developed my patient-facing competencies — from precision prescription verification and drug dispensing to controlled substance inventory management and support for clinical pharmacy teams in patient counselling and medication reconciliation.

Academically, I have authored a comprehensive research review on Hepatoprotective Mechanisms of Turmeric and Curcumin, and led a formulation project developing a Herbal Mosquito Repellent Gel using Aloe Vera, Citronella, Neem, and Turmeric — with full stability, pH, and efficacy evaluation. My scientific communication skills earned me the First Prize at the World Pharmacist Day Poster Competition (2023) at RMLAU — recognition of both research quality and presentation excellence.

I hold an NSQF Level 5 Certification in AI for Pharma from Skill India & LSSSDC (2026), positioning me at the forefront of AI-driven drug discovery, clinical trial optimisation, and digital health transformation. I am actively seeking roles in QC, R&D, or Production where I can apply this combined expertise to drive pharmaceutical quality and innovation.
